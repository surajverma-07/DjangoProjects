E-Waste Recycling Rewards

At our recycling program, we appreciate your efforts to recycle e-waste responsibly. By recycling your e-waste with us, you can earn credit points that can be used in various ways:

Earn Credit Points

Whether you're an individual or a large corporation, you'll receive credit points for every piece of e-waste you recycle with us. These points accumulate over time and can be used for:

  • Discounted rates on electronic products
  • Exclusive coupons from partner shops

Discounted Rates

Use your earned credit points to purchase electronic products at discounted rates. The more you recycle, the more you save on your next tech purchase.

Exclusive Coupons

Our program partners with various shops and retailers. As a member, you'll receive exclusive coupons that can be used for discounts, special offers, and promotions at these partner shops.

Certificate Of Apppreciation

When a user reaches a certain credit point limit like - C.P.1000 , we will give recognition to them by offering them a Certificate of Appreciation which will be authorised by Ministry Of Environment,GOI
